Best Movies According to Jorge

My top 3 favorite movies of all time

Avatar

Extremely beautiful and breathtaking scenery. I wish Pandora was real.

Star Wars: Episode III - Revenge of the Sith

Even though it was tragic and sad to see the fall of Anakin, it was exciting to see the birth of Darth Vader.

Spider-Man: No Way Home

Really cool to see all 3 Spider-Man together in one film.
